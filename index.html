Rick and Morty

Review

Rick and Morty is an American animated television series for adults and teens. Created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon in 2013. The series is about the misadventures of a scientist, Rick Sanchez, and his grandson Morty. It originated as a short film that parodies the characters "Doc and Marty" from the "Back to the Future" trilogy released in 1985.
